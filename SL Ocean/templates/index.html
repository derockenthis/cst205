Surf Rider Foundation

Surf Riders are determined to protect the ocean in order for the public to be able to enjoy it. They do this by tackling issues that threaten beaches and the right for everyone to be able to enjoy beaches. Surf riders also help defend our wild life in the ocean by trying to reduce the amount of plastic in the ocean. They have been very successful with there efforts with a total of 176 chapters and student clubs along with over 182,000 volunteer hours in 2019.

Protecting the World's Oceans

4Ocean

This company was founded by two people who had traveled to Bali and were shocked by the amount of plastic that was found on the beaches. This made them see just how big of a problem the ocean plastic crisis was. They decided to make and sell bracelets that are made from plastic that was pulled from the ocean. For every product that is bought, it helps to remove one pound of trash from the ocean as well as rivers or coastlines.
